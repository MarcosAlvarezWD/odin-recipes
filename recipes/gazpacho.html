Gazpacho


What Is Gazpacho?

Gazpacho is a smooth, blended, cold soup made with fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 4 large fresh tomatoes, peeled and diced
  • ½ cucumber, peeled and finely diced
  • ½ finely diced red pepper
  • ¼ cup onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar

Steps

  1. Combine diced tomatoes, cucumber, pepper, onion, and garlic in a large bowl. Stir in salt and cumin.
  2. Place all with olive oil and vinegar in a blender. Cover and purée until smooth.
  3. Cover and chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
